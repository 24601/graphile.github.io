<MarketingCodebox>

<big>Try it now:</big>

<big><pre><code>npx postgraphile -c my_db --watch</code></pre></big>

<small><em>requires Node.js 8.6 or higher</em></small>

</MarketingCodebox>

If your database is on another machine, you may pass a connection string in place of `my_db`.

Connection strings have the form:

`postgres://pg_user:pg_pass@pg_host:pg_port/pg_db?ssl=1`
